# About ASPIRE Lab

We research **Autonomous Systems using AI for Perception, Intelligence, and Robotic Exploration**.

Focus areas include:

- Legged and aerial robotics  
- Visual perception and machine vision  
- Reinforcement learning and nonlinear control  
- Sim-to-Real transfer for intelligent robotic systems  

Visit us at [IIT Hyderabad](https://iith.ac.in).
